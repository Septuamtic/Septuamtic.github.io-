2026-05-26 thoughts

hello, world.

Every blog starts somewhere. This is where mine starts.

I've wanted a place to write for a while — somewhere that isn't a social feed, doesn't have an algorithm trying to optimise my attention, and doesn't require me to perform. Just a page. My page. You're reading it.

why plain html

I could've used a CMS. I didn't. There's something satisfying about a site that is exactly what it looks like: text files, a stylesheet, and nothing else running behind the scenes. If the internet goes weird in twenty years, these files still work.

Also it's fast. Zero JavaScript frameworks. Zero build steps. Just write, save, push.

The best tool is usually the simplest one that does the job.
